    • We’re thrilled to announce the launch of our first ever art edition, a special collaboration with poet and artist Thomas A. Clark. This is the American edition of an earlier work by Clark for a show in Scotland, re-designed and produced in the US.

    A solid aluminum bar, painted a striking red-orange, and bearing the text “TO PLACE AMONG OTHER COLORS AND FORMS.” Limited to a numbered edition of 25 with accompanying edition card, produced and signed by Clark.
